About Us

Sales of limestone and granite crushed stone with delivery

We supply limestone and granite crushed stone of various fractions for industrial and construction needs. Regular rail deliveries across Ukraine for sugar mills, manufacturing and construction companies.

1

Step

Contact us, indicate the fraction, volume and destination station. We will propose the optimal option and calculate the cost.

2

Step

We will arrange loading at the quarry into wagons and dispatch along your route.

3

Step

We will ensure delivery to the destination station with the full set of accompanying documents.

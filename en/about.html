ABOUT US

TRIUMPH was founded in 2016 as a trading company supplying limestone, non-metallic, and construction materials to industrial and construction enterprises in Ukraine. Due to shrinking sales markets for limestone products, as well as high competition in the construction materials supply market, the company was forced to change its business direction and move away from trading. Thus, in 2021 TRIUMPH focused on activities in the rail freight transportation market. At that time, the rail transportation market was experiencing an acute shortage of rolling stock, so, with significant experience in delivering cargo by rail, the company’s management was able to establish client acquisition and agree on long-term cooperation with enterprises in this sector. As of 2025, TRIUMPH is one of the leading transport and freight forwarding companies in Ukraine, continuously and dynamically developing.

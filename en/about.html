About Us

TPK “Triumph”

LLC TPK “Triumph” is one of the leading rail freight operators in Ukraine. For 8 years on the market we have proven to be a reliable logistics partner for domestic producers. With a fleet of over four hundred gondola cars, we organize your shipments across Ukraine and abroad (Moldova, Romania, Poland, Hungary).

Rapid response

Preparation of required legal documents and sourcing rolling stock with a predictable delivery time to the loading station takes no more than 1 day.

Control and support

Continuous dialogue with shippers, consignees and Ukrainian Railways. Daily email reports with wagon locations. Technical support for document preparation in E-Portal and Mesplan systems.

Flexible pricing policy

Initial market rates agreed at the start of cooperation are reviewed monthly. We often reduce them for partners who load and unload wagons without idle time.
